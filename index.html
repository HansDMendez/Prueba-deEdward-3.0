Popular Phrases

Facebook, Social network.

Facebook is the main social network that exists in the world. A network of virtual links, whose main objective is to support the production and sharing of content. It came to expand the possibilities of social relationships and caused a significant revolution in the world of communications. Marketing was no stranger to this change, finding in this modality fertile ground for new concepts and approaches.

Instagram, Social network.

Instagram is a social network and a mobile application at the same time, which allows its users to upload images and videos with multiple photographic effects such as filters, frames, retro colors, etc., to later share those images on the same platform or on other networks social.

Whasapp, Social network.

WhatsApp is a chat application for mobile phones of the latest generation, the so-called smartphones. It is used to send text and multimedia messages between its users. Its operation is similar to the most common instant messaging programs for computers, although it is focused on and adapted to mobile phones. There is a desktop WhatsApp web for PC and therefore WhatsApp also works on iPad and most tablets.

Spotify, Social network.

Spotify is a digital music, podcast, and video service that gives you access to millions of songs and other content from creators around the world. Basic features like listening to music are completely free, but you also have the option to upgrade to Spotify Premium. Spotify is available on a wide variety of devices, including computers, phones, tablets, speakers, TVs and cars, and you can easily switch between them with Spotify Connect.
